Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that features layers of pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese filling. This hearty and satisfying meal is perfect for family dinners and special occasions. The combination of tender pasta, flavorful ragù, and melted mozzarella creates a comforting dish that appeals to people of all ages.

This traditional recipe brings together simple ingredients like ground beef, tomatoes, and fresh herbs to create a dish that's both elegant and approachable. Whether you're cooking for a crowd or preparing a meal for your family, homemade lasagna is sure to impress with its warm, inviting flavors and satisfying texture.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pound ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 egg
  • 9 lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions, drain, and set aside.
  3. In a large pot, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat if needed.
  4. Add diced onion and minced garlic to the beef, cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese, 1 cup mozzarella cheese, parmesan cheese, and egg. Mix well.
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  8. Layer 3 noodles, then half of the ricotta mixture, then one-third of the remaining meat sauce.
  9. Repeat with 3 more noodles, remaining ricotta mixture, and half of the remaining meat sauce.
  10. Top with final 3 noodles, remaining meat sauce, and sprinkle with remaining 1 cup mozzarella cheese.
  11. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  12. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
